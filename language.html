Karla's Story

In 2007-2008, I went to kindergarten in the bronx,I was so nervous, I didn't know what to expect.Kindergarten was hard work for me because I had to learn English. Everybody in my class didn't speak spanish and if they did I wouldn't understand them (we were 5! obviously).For Example, Every Time I had to go to the bathroom,I had to act it out because if I spoke spanish, they wouldn't understand me.Learning the language was definitely something that I still carry today because I speak english at school so I can communicate with my friends.
