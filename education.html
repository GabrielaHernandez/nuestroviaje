Karla's Story

In the year 2012, I was finally in the 4th grade.4th grade wasn't the best for me because I got bullied. Not bullied because of my body or even my unique traits but because of my race.For example,people would say “That's why you crossed the border” or even “shut up mexican” everytime I got into a argument with somebody else in the playground. I was so young,so I just ignored it but then the name calling got out of control.I started to feel offended.Discrimination is something that a person shouldn't go through , especially at a very young age.
